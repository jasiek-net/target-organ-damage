About Application

This application can be used to assess targen organ damage in children, including:

  • left ventricular mass index (LVMI), calculated according to the Simone formula,
  • common carotid artery intima–media thickness (cIMT),
  • carotid wall cross-sectional area (WCSA) calculated from the equation: WCSA=π*(dD/2+IMT)2−π(dD/2)2, where dD is the mean diastolic diameter.
  • aortic pulse pressure (AoBP) and pulse wave velocity (PWV), obtained using non-invasive measurement of Vicorder device.

All calculations are based on current values. For more details go to jonzee.me/tod. If you have any suggestions or questions, write to me: jan.horubala@gmail.com

References:

  3. Elmenhorst J, Hulpke-Wette M, Barta C i wsp. Percentiles for central blood pressure and pulse wave velocity in children and adolescents recorded with an oscillometric device. Atherosclerosis. 2015;238:9-16.
